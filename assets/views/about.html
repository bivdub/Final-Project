Mood Mining

Our goal with Mood Mining was to create engaging visualization tools for subjective and somewhat abstract data sets. We used Twitter, LastFM, and Weather API’s, filtered by region. After amassing our data, we passed all of our ‘feelings’ through an algorithm to evaluate the overall positive or negative score of each term. To create our app, we used Angular and Sails with D3 and NVD3 for creating our data visualizations.
